What Is A Virtual Classroom?

As the learning experience has been enhanced with new technologies like e-learning, e-exams, the traditional classroom-led method becomes an old school concept. Traditional bench, blackboard, and tutors moving through the benches are replaced by virtual or online sessions. In these sessions, tutors and learners are interconnected through the internet, and the online learning environment is called virtual classrooms. Students connect with teachers anywhere, anytime through interactive online platform virtually. New tools like online whiteboards, video conferencing, participation controls, instant messaging are needs for this new-age learning method. These online classrooms provide an abundance of opportunities especially when embedded with school management systems. Virtual classrooms break the synchronous hurdles of location, place, student limits and cater the knowledge to the learning crowd.
